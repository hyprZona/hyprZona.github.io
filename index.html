Who the holy duck am I?

⚠ Warning: This bio contains broken humor, unapologetic vulgarity, and enough sarcasm to drown your last brain cell. If you’re easily offended, this is your cue to exit faster than your dad left for cigarettes. Proceed with caution—or don’t. I’m not your mom.

Let’s get this disaster rolling.

I’m Daksh Mehta, your friendly neighborhood chaos engine. Call me Dakie, Ducky (no, I don’t waddle or swim laps), or that squishy-ass momo that somehow survived life’s steam pot. Cute? Sure. Tragic? Definitely. But let’s not sugarcoat it—I’m a walking combo meal of ADHD, depression, and social anxiety, with a side of I’m fine lies. My brain’s got more plot twists than a telenovela about alien ducks invading Earth.

My Passions (or lack thereof):

I’m your classic sci-fi, action-movie-junkie-with-a-side-of-broke. Disney+? Yup. Prime? Absolutely. Netflix? Nah, I’m a man of humble subscriptions, thank you very much. In another life, I’d be Gordon Ramsay, minus the kitchen and the British accent. All I need is an oven and a brain that doesn’t ADHD itself into oblivion every two minutes.

If I Were a Flavor:

Tangy tomato chips with an existential crisis. Sweet, spicy, and sour enough to make you rethink your life choices. Basically, I’m the human version of pineapple on pizza—a divisive asshole who’ll either ruin your vibe or become your guilty pleasure. Speaking of pineapple on pizza: no. That’s the culinary equivalent of nut-kicking a chef and spitting on their soufflé. Even zombies would gag at that combo.

Speaking of Zombies...

If I ever get chased, hand me a guitar and a potato, and it’s showtime. I’d try to strum a killer tune (using the potato as a pick, of course) and hope it unleashes some magical death notes. Knowing my luck, though, I’d end up playing something so awful the zombies would stop, give me a pitying blowjob (no, not that kind, you little gremlin), and shuffle off in disappointment.

Ah, yes—Straight Hero to Zero moments.

Like the time in school when I tried to save a girl from falling. Heroic, right? Wrong. My dumb ass grabbed her… by the ass. Hard. Turns out, I’m a walking HR violation. Her scream was louder than a jet engine blasting through hell. I stood there wishing I could Thanos-snap myself into oblivion. Luckily, nobody called a 9/11 on me, so here I am. Classic Dakie Moment™.

On My “IDGAF” Attitude:

Experiences like that built my “IDGAF” attitude, so toxic it could be bottled and sold as poison. Life’s too short to care about Karen’s complaints or Chad’s fragile ego. I’ll do my thing, and if you don’t like it? Go fuck yourself with a rusty spoon.

My favorite emojis? 🫡, 🤨, and 💀—because they say everything I need without me wasting my breath. If vibes were a religion, I’d be their nihilistic prophet.

Aliens and Rockstar Games:

If aliens ever invite me for a ride, I’m gone. Earth, it’s been real. I’ll come back with all the alien secrets and rebrand myself as Mr. Newton-who-fucked-Einstein, with the ropes used to tie Jesus as a cherry on top. All that just to infiltrate Rockstar Games’ servers and finally find out what the hell they’re cooking. Don’t worry, though—I’m not out here trying to be the next Arion Kurtaj. Rockstar deserves respect… just not my wallet.

In a Nutshell:

I’m “Given Up”, followed by “Points of Authority”, and topped with “Bubbles” from Linkin Park’s Mmm...Cookies album—all delivered with the Static Max Payne Smile. Stare at me when I’m alone, and you’ll see a blank, haunting expression hiding a million inappropriate thoughts. Like the time I wrote the most cursed lyric ever: You so hot, I want to tie you on my bed like Jesus on the cross. My friends found it, and let’s just say I won’t be showing my face in heaven anytime soon.

Final Toppings:

Toss in some ADHD (since day one), depression (it’s not a bug; it’s a feature), and social anxiety for seasoning, and you’ve got the ultimate snack… or disaster.

Welcome to My Circus 🎪

Grab some popcorn, dodge the flying fucks, and enjoy the ride. Life’s a joke, and I’m the punchline. Signing off 🫡

Who We Are

Welcome to hyprZona, where creativity strikes like lightning! Founded with a dream of delivering unparalleled experiences in the indie games industry, hyprZona is the brainchild of a one-boy powerhouse with an insatiable love for storytelling, immersive gameplay, and bold experimentation.

Our Mission

At hyprZona, we don’t just create games, eBooks, or music—we craft experiences. Our mission is to bridge the gap between creators and audiences, fostering a unique, transparent development process. We believe in keeping the creative doors open, regularly updating you on our projects, and even inviting your ideas for gameplay mechanics. After all, what’s better than an indie creator who actually listens?

The Name and Philosophy

Let’s talk about the name, because we know you’re curious. hyprZona doesn’t just roll off the tongue; it zaps with meaning. “Hypr” stands for hyper (stylishly, of course), and “Z” symbolizes thunder—a perfect match for our neon-soaked aesthetics. Sure, there are similarly named companies, but hey, originality isn’t always about being the first. It’s about being the most memorable (the one, the "ona").

What Sets Us Apart

Quality over quantity—that’s our mantra. From deeply detailed narratives to choices that keep you coming back for more, hyprZona takes pride in creating games that resonate. Each story, mechanic, and visual is crafted with the care of someone who’s obsessed with delivering only the best. Plus, we’re not shy about sharing behind-the-scenes moments, screenshots, and quirky updates (but we’ll keep some secrets—you don’t want all the spoilers, do you?).

Our Offerings

  • Games: At the heart of hyprZona lies the pulse of action, dark/noir, and horror. But we’re not afraid to step into fantasy, satire, or puzzle genres, because creativity knows no bounds.
  • eBooks: Every now and then, the pen takes over. If it strikes a chord, it strikes the paper.
  • Music: What’s a story without the right melody? Music happens when inspiration calls, and when it does, we answer.

Achievements and Inspirations

Our flagship eBook, The T’N’T, stands out as a vivid, emotionally intense masterpiece. As for our first game prototype, Beyond the Illusions... well, let’s just say we call it “humble beginnings.” (We’re all about the glow-up!)

Behind hyprZona is a boy who started out reverse-engineering RC cars and experimenting with science books as a kid before transitioning to writing, music, and game development. With a journey that began as a Discord bot developer and evolved into creating story-driven games, the passion to bring imagination to life has been the guiding force.

Culture and Vibes

hyprZona thrives on a mix of intense focus and lighthearted goofiness. Easter eggs, quirky processes, and behind-the-scenes antics are part of the DNA. From the absurd aftermath of brainstorming sessions to moments of sheer brilliance, everything we do is about balancing hard work with the joy of creation.

Fun Facts to Zap You

  • Our first planned game, Memories, was shelved temporarily because... well, we were still learning what buttons to press.
  • hyprZona almost became just an eBook company, but discovering Godot on a potato laptop changed everything.
  • Every misstep and goofy behind-the-scenes moment will live on as easter eggs in future games—because why not laugh at yourself?
