Beyond The Illusions

Story

Beyond The Illusions is a unique FP adventure where reality and nightmares intertwine. Step into the shoes of a heartbroken young man who, after an unexpected incident, finds himself lost in a mysterious forest filled with haunting secrets and dark surprises.

Your journey begins with a bike ride that quickly takes a chilling turn as you struggle to piece together your fragmented memories. Navigate through eerie woods, uncover lost memories, and choose your path wisely — every decision shapes your story.

Gameplay Features

  • Basic FPS mechanics: Ride, run, and explore in first-person perspective.
  • Memory Retrieval: Uncover hidden memories scattered throughout a dark forest.
  • Multiple Endings: Choose your path — enter or avoid the cave, run or confront the monsters.
  • Survival Challenges: Face environmental obstacles, from navigating a lava river to avoiding traps on a zipline.
  • Dynamic Obstacles: Experience random effects like sudden blackouts, freezes, and changes in movement speed.

Additional Information

This game is a playable testbed and prototype, serving as a learning curve for the developer. It offers a unique horror-survival-adventure experience available on web, Windows, and MacOS platforms.
