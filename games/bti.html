Beyond The Illusions

Depression, manifestation, and illusions lead the player to a new and different dimension.

Game Details

  • Release Date: 5th September 2024
  • Playtime: Approx. 30 mins.
  • Price: Free
  • Platform:
  • State: Released
  • Stage: v1
  • Genre: Psychological Horror, Action-Adventure, Survival

Summary

Embark on a dark, psychological journey as a young man, lost in the eerie depths of the 'Mid City Mountain' Forest. Struggling with a broken heart, he sets off on a seemingly harmless trip to clear his mind, only to find himself trapped in a world where reality and illusion blur. As night falls, shadows grow longer, and the forest reveals its true, sinister nature. Can he escape this twisted place, or will he be consumed by its mysteries?

Story

On his birthday, a young man, emotionally devastated by a sudden breakup, decides to seek solace in the mountains. What begins as a simple hike turns into something far more sinister. Guided by fragmented memories and fleeting moments of clarity, he navigates a forest that seems to change at every turn. Strange phenomena haunt his every step — a hit to the head leaves him with amnesia, the forest shifts into a dark, haunting place, and he discovers a mysterious cave that hides more than it reveals.

Each memory he retrieves alters his perception of the world around him, challenging his understanding of reality. As he ventures deeper into the unknown, encountering enigmatic figures and dangerous obstacles, he must confront the blurred lines between his imagination and the dark truth of the forest. The decisions he makes will shape his destiny in this psychological thriller, where every choice matters.

Gameplay

Beyond The Illusions combines immersive storytelling with dynamic FPS gameplay:

  1. Navigate through forest paths while cycling, dodging obstacles and avoiding collisions.
  2. Explore and uncover memory fragments, using cryptic hints to piece together lost memories.
  3. Face challenges like sudden blackouts, frozen movement, and disorienting forces that test your wits and reflexes.
  4. Engage in decision-making that influences the course of the story, such as whether to enter a mysterious cave or stay on the safer path.
  5. Choose your actions carefully when encountering threats — fight, flee, or surrender.
  6. Experience thrilling chases, navigating through complex terrain as you evade a relentless pursuer.
  7. Overcome treacherous landscapes like molten rivers by jumping across unstable platforms.
  8. Control a zipline descent with precision, dodging obstacles in a nerve-wracking aerial sequence.
