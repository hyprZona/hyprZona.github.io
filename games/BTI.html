Beyond The Illusions

Where the reality mixes with illusions.

Summary

Embark on a chilling journey into the mind of a young boy grappling with depression. When a routine visit to a forest turns into a surreal nightmare, he must navigate twisted realities and dark secrets. Will he find the strength to escape, or will he succumb to the horrors that lurk within?
