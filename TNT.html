The T'N'T:Prologue

A noir tale of two undercover agents shaped by love, loss, and vengeance.

About the Book

Step into the gritty past of Tom and Tim, two agents with different paths but united by fate. Unravel the mysteries that forged them, one story at a time.

Story Structure

The narrative unfolds through multiple perspectives, primarily focusing on our two protagonists and a narrator. The story is divided into intro, three parts and post-credits:

  1. Introduction: The foundation of all mysteries, setting the stage for the story.
  2. Part I (Tom's Prologue): Tom's journey through tragedy, revenge, and love, starting with his parents' death by a drug mafia and leading to his resolve as an officer.
  3. Part II (Tim's Prologue): A peek into Tim's darker past—his fight against oppression and his evolution into a cool, calm, and practical agent.
  4. Part III (Finale): The duo’s most intense mission involving a deadly high-tech weapon and a cliffhanger ending that ties back to their shared fate.
  5. Post-Credits: A final twist that leaves readers pondering more.
