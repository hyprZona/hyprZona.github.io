The T'N'T: Prologue

Explore the dark and tragic past of two undercover agents, Tom and Tim, in a noir tale filled with love, loss, and revenge.

About the Book

Dive into the thrilling journey of Tom & Tim's duo, both with similar stories but opposite perspectives.
Uncover interesting stories of their past filled with Tragedy, Action, Morals, Love & Dark Realities.
The Story Feature a tri-narration: Tom (global except Tim's Prologue), Tim (only Tim's Prologue) & The Narrator (Global)
The Story is divided into Introduction, Part - I, Part - II, Part - III & a post credits.
Introduction: A short introduction of the protagonist duo, in a situation that lays the foundation of the whole prologue.
Part I: Is all about Tom his past, learn about his parents, his love and what drove him to be an officer.
Part II: Following a conversation from the previous part we get to know about Tim's past from his perspective, a dark past that merges with reality, find out how he faced it and what shaped him to be who he is in present.
Part III: Experience the most intense mission Tom & Tim had to accomplish, filled with adrenaline pumping action, suspense and a showdown chase.
Post-Credits: It is more than just being credits & a thank you, it leaves you with something incomplete to brainstorm on!
So read it now & experience the action, adventure & thrill with our Duo.
