Our Musical Landscape

Fading Ashes is a budding music division founded with a passion for blending the raw intensity of rock, nu-metal, and indie-alternative with themes that resonate deeply with its audience. Drawing inspiration from legends like Linkin Park, Dead by Sunrise, and Dead Sara, Fading Ashes crafts music that offers a sense of hope, unity, and emotional connection, while occasionally weaving in powerful storytelling.

With roots in game soundtracks, the division's debut includes two immersive instrumental pieces for an 8-minute-long project. Fading Ashes aims to evolve, producing standalone tracks and a future EP or album filled with relatable lyrics and a signature style. Promising interactive Q&A sessions and music inspired by audience feedback, Fading Ashes stands as a beacon for those seeking solace and connection through sound.
