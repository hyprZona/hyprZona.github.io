About Us

Fading Ashes, a subdivision of hyprZona, was founded by the 16-year-old D.M. Our music is heavily inspired by Linkin Park, focusing on the evolution of music, style, and lyrics. We primarily create dark romance and life lesson-themed songs, with occasional light-hearted compositions.
