About Fading Ashes

Fading Ashes is a solo music project that blends the raw energy of rock with the catchy hooks of pop. Drawing inspiration from iconic bands like Linkin Park, I create music that's both dark and funky, exploring themes of resilience and transformation.

As a subdivision of hyprZona, Fading Ashes pushes the boundaries of contemporary rock and pop, delivering powerful melodies and hard-hitting lyrics that resonate with listeners.
