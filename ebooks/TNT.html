The T'N'T

A Story of Scars

Two men, two scars, one explosive fight against a world that left them broken.

Synopsis

Set in a modern-day fictional nation with a past marred by loss and corruption, The T'N'T: A Story of Them Scars is an emotionally charged noir that delves into the intertwined lives of two undercover agents, Tom Martin and Tim Darkens. Bound by tragedy and driven by justice, they stand against a nation overtaken by greed-fueled drug lords and a corrupt system that feeds off the suffering of its people.

Tom, the idealistic offspring of a former president, and Tim, a war survivor hardened by life’s cruelties, unite their contrasting strengths to dismantle a powerful syndicate. While their missions are deadly serious, lighthearted moments of camaraderie, romance, and pop-culture quips weave humanity into their journey. As the duo navigates urban streets and slum back alleys, they uncover not only the depths of corruption but also the unbreakable bond that fuels their resolve.

Their scars—both physical and emotional—tell a story of loss, resilience, and the cost of fighting for a better tomorrow.
